# mint.fun

> Second product in the [[Context (the team/company)]] arc; the **only commercial success** in the arc — acquired by [[Zora]] in 2024.

## Concept

NFT discovery + minting aggregation UX. Solved the "where do I mint this" problem by indexing active mints across Ethereum / Base / Zora / other chains and giving a clean one-click mint interface with gas estimation, rewards tracking, and social discovery.

## Key facts

- **Team:** Luke Miles (co-founder) + rest of [[Context (the team/company)]]
- **Scale:** Handled **millions of monthly mints** at peak
- **Acquirer:** [[Zora]]
- **Acquisition year:** 2024
- **Rationale from Zora's side:** cemented Zora's position as "creator-first ecosystem" — the discovery layer + minting UX folded into Zora's upstream content-coin protocol

## Mechanism / surfaces

- **Feed / trending:** hot mints surfaced with real-time data
- **Social layer:** see what your connections are minting
- **Aggregation:** abstract away gas / wallet clicks / network switching
- **Rewards:** referral-style incentives for surfacing popular mints (influence the flywheel that [[Rodeo]] later formalized)

## Why this was the winner (commercially) in Context's arc

- **Clear economic hook:** platform fees on every mint — every transaction monetized
- **Flywheel:** more mints → better discovery → more mints
- **Acquisition target:** upstream protocol (Zora) needed the UX/discovery layer; downstream aggregator had real users → clean M&A fit
