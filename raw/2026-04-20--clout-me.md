# Clout (clout.me)

## User thesis (Alex)

> Solana creator coins.

## Research summary

Clout was an early Solana SocialFi app from founder **Ben Pasternak** — users could buy tokenized versions of social-media profiles, echoing the BitClout → friend.tech lineage. Downloads spiked after the iPhone app launched.

Clout subsequently became **[[Believe (believe.app)]]** — same founder, same core "tokenize social accounts" thesis, evolved into a Twitter-mention launchpad. In practice, "Clout" and "Believe" are two phases of the same project.

## Key facts

- **Founder:** Ben Pasternak
- **Chain:** Solana
- **Lineage:** BitClout (2021) → friend.tech (2023) → Clout → Believe.app
- **Status (as of research):** superseded by believe.app
