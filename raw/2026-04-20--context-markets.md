# context.markets

> Fifth and final product of the [[Context (the team/company)]] team. Prediction-markets platform. Shut down with the company in 2026.

## Concept

Prediction markets — the final product in the Context arc. The evolution: [[meme.market]] was prediction markets on meme coins specifically; context.markets broadened the scope (presumably general-purpose markets on events, outcomes, pop culture).

## Status

Shut down with the company in 2026 per the team's announcement ("we're saying goodbye to Context Markets and Context, the company").

## Key facts

- **Team:** [[Context (the team/company)]] — Luke Miles co-founder
- **Product type:** prediction market
- **Status:** sunset

## Why this matters (vs Polymarket / Kalshi)

- Context's final pivot shows **the same arc as [[Foundation Labs]]**: NFT/social company → prediction-market product → wind-down
- Two independent teams converging on prediction-markets as the end-state of the "consumer crypto" thesis is a signal worth tracking
- Both hit a wall: **Polymarket and Kalshi captured the attention + liquidity** that an independent mid-sized prediction-markets app needed to compete
