# Zora

## User thesis (Alex)

> Specifically their IG-like app with the **token liquidity flywheel content/creator/ZORA coins**.

The load-bearing observation: Zora's innovation isn't "posts are coins" (others did that) — it's the **three-coin nested flywheel** where each layer provides liquidity for the layer above it. Content coins pair with creator coins, creator coins pair with ZORA. Every new post deepens every preceding pool in the creator's hierarchy.

## Research summary

Zora started as an NFT marketplace/protocol (pre-2023), pivoted to a **Zora Network L2** + **Coins protocol**, and now runs a consumer mobile app where **every post is a tradeable ERC-20 coin**. Visual feed, IG-style UX, but every piece of content is a tradeable asset.

## The three-coin flywheel

| Layer | What it is | Pairs with |
|---|---|---|
| **Content coin** | ERC-20 per individual post | paired with its creator's **Creator Coin** |
| **Creator coin** | ERC-20 per user profile | paired with **$ZORA** |
| **$ZORA** | Platform token | paired with USD/ETH at the base |

Every time someone posts, a new content coin is created and automatically LPed against the creator's coin. Every new creator coin is LPed against $ZORA. The hierarchy compounds liquidity upward.

## Key facts

- **Zora Network L2:** OP Stack chain, launched 2023
- **$ZORA token launch:** April 23, 2025
- **Q2 2025 stats:** $353M trading volume, $27M paid to creators, 179k creators, 2.8M traders
- **Cumulative:** 1.6M coins created across 200k+ creators, $445M+ volume
- **Coinbase Base App integration:** July 17, 2025 — Base App bundled Zora's tokenization with [[Farcaster]]'s social graph

## Fees

- **3% per trade**, split three ways:
  - 1.0% → creator
  - 1.0% → Zora protocol
  - 1.0% → liquidity providers
- Creators earn **50% of trade fees in $ZORA** for tokenized posts
- Creators need $ZORA to launch a Creator Coin → demand sink for ZORA token

## Notable features

- **Native post-as-coin:** no user action required to tokenize; every post is automatically a coin
- **Instant LP:** protocol auto-creates and seeds the liquidity pool
- **Integration with Base App:** reached millions of wallets via Coinbase distribution
- **Flywheel design:** each layer subsidizes the next — ZORA benefits from every content trade

## Why the flywheel matters (Alex's angle)

Most SocialFi apps have one liquidity venue (keys on friend.tech, SPL tokens on pump.fun). Zora stacks them: holding a creator coin gives you exposure to all their future content coins via the pairing. This means **curators (early creator-coin buyers) benefit from creator's subsequent posting activity** — a structural version of the [[Rodeo]] "collect-to-earn for known curators" insight, but baked into the asset structure rather than relying on referrals.
