# Drakula (drakula.app)

## Alex's framing

> **DEGEN-flywheel bonding curve for Farcaster creator in a video app.**

## Parsing

Three overlapping mechanics in one product:
1. **Video app** — short-form video (TikTok-adjacent format)
2. **Bonding curve for Farcaster creator** — friend.tech-style per-creator tokens, but native to the [[Farcaster]] graph (identity = Farcaster FID/username)
3. **DEGEN flywheel** — integration with the **DEGEN tipping / airdrop economy** on [[Base]], which was Farcaster's native "tip your friends with meme money" token

So: video content by Farcaster creators → each creator has a bonding-curve-priced token → DEGEN tips compound into the curve. The flywheel: watch → tip in DEGEN → appreciation of creator's bonding-curve coin → more attention on creator → more tips.

## Positioning in the space

- Hybrid of [[friend.tech]] (bonding curves on profiles) + [[TikTok]]-style video UX + [[Farcaster]]-native identity
- **Farcaster-first** not chain-first — leveraged Farcaster's verified-identity graph vs re-implementing social primitives
- **DEGEN integration** was the differentiator — leaned into an emergent Farcaster-culture token rather than inventing a new one

## Why this matters for the wiki

- A clear **"stack on Farcaster's graph rather than build one"** example
- Useful contrast to [[Giggles]] (video + prediction market on virality) — Drakula was video + bonding curve on creators, Giggles is video + prediction market on videos
- DEGEN-flywheel integration is itself a pattern: using an **existing meme token's attention as the fuel** rather than launching your own
