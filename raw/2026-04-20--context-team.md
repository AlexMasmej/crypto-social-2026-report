# Context (the team/company)

## User thesis (Alex)

Alex dropped this into the wiki on 2026-04-20 with a shutdown announcement from the team. The **5-product arc** itself is the thesis: Context's history is a retrospective on 2021–2026 consumer-crypto product hunting. Each product is a hypothesis about "how to make web3 more fun" that taught the team something before the next bet.

## Shutdown announcement (verbatim)

> oh yes context.markets! We started Context in 2021 with a simple goal: make web3 more fun. Today, we're saying goodbye to Context Markets and Context, the company.
>

## Core thesis across products

"Make web3 more fun." Every product is a hypothesis about where fun lives: activity feeds → minting UX → gamified launches → speculation markets → event markets. Pattern: each pivot moved from *ownership* toward *speculation*.

## Why this matters (Alex's angle / research note)

The Context team shows up in the same part of the venn diagram as [[Foundation Labs]] (NFT marketplace → social app → prediction market / Aura) — both teams iterated through **NFT → social → prediction-markets**, suggesting the consumer-crypto product space has a discoverable gradient that converges on speculation-as-entertainment.

Both companies winding down / consolidating in Q1 2026 is a pattern worth tracking in wiki/concepts/.
